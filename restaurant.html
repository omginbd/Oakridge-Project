From the Bar and Grill, overlooking one of the most beautiful golf courses in Utah, members and guests can enjoy gourmet dining and an impressive view of the majestic Wasatch Mountains. The menu is varied and features exquisite seasonal selections.

The dining room, with its fireplace and comfortable seating is the perfect place for friends to gather and relax in a pleasant atmosphere.
